I'm Colin Szechy, an undergrad at the University of Michigan.

I love hacking on various projects from embedded systems to smartphone apps and in-between. My favorite projects are in the ubiquitous computing realm, or helping others learn about various parts of computer science and engineering. Recently, I've become fascinated by the interactions between public policy, technology, and culture.

I'm involved with organizing MHacks and Michigan Hackers, and I'm a student instructor for EECS 373 - Microprocessor-Based Systems. I've worked with the Michigan Embedded System Hub, Michigan Embedded Systems Lab, and Formula SAE on-campus, and National Instruments and Fiat Chrysler off-campus. I participated in the FIRST Robotics Competition with Team 2337.

You can contact me at @cszechy or by email.
